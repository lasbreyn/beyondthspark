There are three kinds of creators in every act of creation. The Originator, who imagines what does not yet exist. The Extender, who makes the imagined real. The Multiplier, who sees what else the real thing could become.

Most of us are exceptional at one of the three roles and decent at one other. The trouble is, almost nobody has ever taught us to see this clearly. The quiz that follows will.

Twenty questions. Roughly ten minutes. For each question, pick the statement that is more true of you — even if neither feels perfectly accurate. Trust your first instinct.
